No one is escaping the national hops shortage, not even us. With the nations hops crop suffering from the freezing temperatures still affecting the north, our supply of hops is beginning to dry low. Brewers use copper too

4-8-2011

The price of copper doesn't just affect your computer, it also affects the brewers. With the price of copper on the rise, the copper fermenters we use to brew our a beers are getting more and more difficult to find. And since our San Francisco Brewery looking to add more brewing capacity, we'll have to keep our eyes to find good deals. If one of our patrons happens to find a deal, don't hesitate to let us know.
