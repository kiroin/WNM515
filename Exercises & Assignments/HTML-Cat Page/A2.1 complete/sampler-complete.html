Tutorial: How to draw a cat

Why learn to draw a cat?

Cats are super popular on the internet- you want to show off you’re hip to the times, right? Also, you never know when you will be in an emergency game of pictionary. Don’t let your team down.

Things you need

  1. Draw a circle for the head
  2. Add ears - triangles
  3. Add Eyes - 2 small circles
  4. Add Nose - a downard triangle
  5. Mouth - 3 curved lines
  6. Wiskers - lines out from the cheeks

Tips on craft

Good examples

  • Mouth points up - makes a happy smile
  • Eyes are balances and even

Bad craft

  • No wiskers, could be mistaken for a dog
  • Lumpy head - looks squished
